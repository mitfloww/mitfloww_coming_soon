How to Send Files After Payment

The safest freelance workflow is simple: payment first, file access later.

Most freelancers still send files manually after payment confirmation. That creates delays, confusion, and risk.

The problem with manual delivery

  • Clients ask for files before paying
  • Payment confirmations get delayed
  • Files get shared accidentally

Better workflow

Upload → Lock → Share link → Auto-unlock after payment

Why this works

This removes human error and ensures payment is part of the delivery system—not a follow-up task.
