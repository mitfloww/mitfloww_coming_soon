Payment workflow

Get Paid Before You Deliver

The most reliable way freelancers can avoid non-payment is to control access before final files are handed over.

If you freelance long enough, this pattern shows up.

You finish the work. You send the files. Then the client says payment is coming soon, and the conversation slows down.

Why this happens

Once the client has full access to the final files, your leverage drops sharply. The work is already delivered, so payment becomes easier to postpone.

What freelancers usually try

  • Watermarked previews
  • Partial deliveries
  • Trust-based follow-ups

These reduce risk, but they do not solve the underlying problem.

The better workflow

Payment first → file access later

That simple structure keeps the process fair and removes the need for repeated reminders.

What changes when payment is enforced

  • Less chasing
  • Fewer awkward follow-ups
  • Clearer client expectations
