Get Paid Before You Deliver

The most reliable way freelancers can avoid non-payment and protect their work.

If you're a freelancer, you've likely faced this situation:

You complete the work. You send the files. Then… silence.

The client disappears, delays payment, or worse—never responds again.

Why this happens

Once a client has full access to your files, you lose leverage.

At that point, payment becomes optional—for them.

Common approaches freelancers try

  • Watermarked previews
  • Partial delivery
  • Trust-based workflows

These methods reduce risk—but don’t eliminate it.

The real solution

The only reliable approach is simple:

No payment → No file access

How modern freelancers handle this

Instead of sending files directly, they use controlled delivery systems where:

  • Clients must pay first
  • Files unlock after payment
  • No chasing required

Final thought

If you're still sending final files before payment, you're taking unnecessary risk.
