What is MitFloww?

MitFloww is a secure file sharing platform that allows freelancers to get paid before clients access their files.

Last updated: April 2026

The problem it solves

Freelancers often deliver work before receiving payment. Once files are shared, there is no control — clients can delay, negotiate, or disappear entirely.

Traditional tools like file sharing platforms only handle delivery. They do not protect the payment workflow.

How MitFloww works

1. Upload your files

Share your work securely through MitFloww.

2. Set payment requirement

Clients must complete payment before accessing files.

3. Client gets access after payment

Files unlock only after payment is confirmed.

Why it’s different

  • No more chasing payments
  • No need for watermarks or partial delivery
  • No reliance on trust
  • Works independently of freelance platforms

Who it’s for

MitFloww is built for freelancers and creators who deliver digital work — including designers, developers, video editors, photographers, and agencies.
